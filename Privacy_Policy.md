# Privacy Policy — Iqamah

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-01  
**App Name:** Iqamah — Prayer Times  
**Platform:** macOS  
**Developer:** Kamal Syed  
**Contact:** ksyed0@gmail.com

---

## Summary

Iqamah does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app needs to function lives entirely on your device.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

Iqamah does **not** collect, use, or transmit:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device ID)
- Location data to any server or third party
- Usage data, analytics, or crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data whatsoever to any external server

---

## 2. Location Data

Iqamah requests permission to access your device's location **only** to identify the nearest city from its local database and calculate accurate Islamic prayer times. This is done entirely on-device:

- Your location coordinates are **never** sent off the device
- Location data is used in memory at runtime only
- No location history is stored
- If you deny location permission, you can still use the app by selecting your city manually from the built-in cities list

---

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

Iqamah saves the following information locally using Apple's `UserDefaults` system, which stays on your Mac and is never transmitted:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Selected city name and coordinates | Calculate prayer times for your location |
| Selected calculation method | Determine Fajr and Isha angles |
| Selected Asr jurisprudence | Determine Asr shadow factor |
| Per-prayer minute adjustments | Show your preferred adjusted times |
| Setup completion flag | Skip onboarding on subsequent launches |

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Iqamah does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, crash reporters, or cloud services. There are no network requests of any kind.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Iqamah does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. No personal data is collected by this app.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because Iqamah collects no data and makes no network connections, there is no data transmission to secure. All settings are stored in Apple's standard sandboxed `UserDefaults`, subject to the security protections of macOS.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Given that the app collects no data, material changes are unlikely.
