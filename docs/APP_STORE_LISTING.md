## Description (4000 characters max)

Iqamah is a clean, accurate prayer times app for Muslims on macOS. It lives quietly in your menu bar showing the next prayer and countdown, so you never miss a prayer.

**Prayer Times**
Iqamah calculates Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha using proven astronomical algorithms — no internet connection required. Prayer times update automatically with the Hijri and Gregorian date displayed side by side.

**Menu Bar Integration**
The next prayer and its time appear directly in your menu bar. The text turns red when a prayer is within 10 minutes, giving you a gentle visual reminder without interrupting your work. Click to open the full prayer times view; right-click for quick options.

**Six Calculation Methods**
Choose the method recognized in your region:
• Muslim World League (MWL)
• Islamic Society of North America (ISNA)
• Egyptian General Authority of Survey
• Umm Al-Qura University, Makkah
• University of Islamic Sciences, Karachi
• Institute of Geophysics, University of Tehran

**Asr Jurisprudence**
Select Standard (Shafi'i, Maliki, Hanbali) or Hanafi for Asr calculation.

**Per-Prayer Adjustments**
Fine-tune any prayer time by ±minutes to match your local mosque or personal preference. Adjustments are saved and reflected in both the prayer times view and the menu bar.

**Qiblah Compass**
A beautiful compass shows the exact bearing to the Ka'bah in Makkah, with your prayer mat and the Ka'bah icon on the compass ring.

**Hijri Calendar**
Both the Gregorian and Hijri dates are shown at the top of the prayer times view.

**Your Location, Your Privacy**
Iqamah detects your nearest city automatically using your GPS location — entirely on-device. No data is ever sent anywhere. You can also select your city manually from the built-in global cities database.

**This app is free. No ads. No subscriptions. No in-app purchases.**
